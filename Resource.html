Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of using MeetMe?

Unlike others, MeetMe is completely FREE!

Can I use MeetMe on my phone?

While MeetMe is a web-based application, it compatible with Apple and Andriod products.

Can I change the links after I create a page?

Absolutely! MeetMe allows our users to return to their home page and edit the links they have added.

How come some links do not work?

In order to make sure our users' data is secure we require websites using HTTPS at the beginning of the URL.
